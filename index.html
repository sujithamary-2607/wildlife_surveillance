Help & Guidelines

🌿 For Tourists

  • Use Image/Video detection to safely identify wildlife from a distance
  • Maintain safe distance from wild animals - never approach them
  • Report any suspicious activity or injured animals to forest authorities
  • Safety levels: SAFE (green), WARNING (orange), DANGER (red)

🛡️ For Admins

  • Monitor real-time threat alerts in the Alerts section
  • Export logs for data analysis and reporting
  • Manage admin accounts through User Management
  • Configure notification settings for instant alerts

⚠️ Danger Indicators

The system will alert for:

  • Poaching activities and hunting equipment
  • Dangerous predators (tigers, lions, snakes, etc.)
  • Deforestation and illegal logging
  • Human-wildlife conflict situations

📸 Tips for Best Results

  • Upload clear, well-lit images for accurate identification
  • Videos should be stable and focused on the subject
  • Use the live camera feature for real-time monitoring
  • Location services help track wildlife movement patterns
